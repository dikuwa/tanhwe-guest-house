# Design style guide

The public register is image-led hospitality; the admin register is restrained operational UI. Orange `#E89008` signals commitment and active actions. Blue `#0D5CA8` guides navigation, links, and informational accents. Warm cream and sand carry most surfaces. Never make orange and blue compete at equal intensity.

Inter Tight is used for every heading, display treatment, card title, and the wordmark. Inter is used for body copy, forms, tables, labels, and navigation. Body prose should remain near 65–75 characters. Corners are moderate, borders fine, and shadows rare. Use semantic status labels with text, not color alone. Motion must be brief, state-oriented, and reduced-motion safe.

Avoid gradients, glass effects, decorative blobs, giant empty heroes, repeated icon-card grids, heavy shadows, and decorative charts.
