# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Travellers, families, business guests, and local organisations looking for dependable accommodation or conference space in Mukwe, Namibia. Visitors are often comparing rooms quickly on a phone and want clear prices, dates, contact options, and a low-friction enquiry rather than an account.

## Product Purpose

Tanhwe Guest House combines a warm public booking website with a practical hospitality administration system. Success means visitors can understand the offer, check availability, and send a useful booking request, while the owner can manage inventory and follow up without editing code.

## Brand Personality

Warm, trustworthy, calm, clean, professional, hospitable, and distinctly local. The emotional goal is the quiet confidence of arriving somewhere well cared for.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS landing pages
- AI-generated card grids, decorative blobs, and oversized empty heroes
- Dark luxury-hotel clichés that hide useful information
- Toy-like admin templates
- Hardcoded room content presented as live inventory

## Design Principles

1. Show the stay: credible room imagery and useful details come before marketing language.
2. Make contact effortless: availability, WhatsApp, and phone actions remain obvious on every device.
3. Earn trust with clarity: prices, inclusions, dates, and enquiry status are explicit.
4. Keep local warmth practical: personality comes through color, imagery, and language without decorative excess.
5. Design for handover: content and workflows stay understandable to a small hospitality team.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.1 AA. Maintain keyboard navigation, visible focus states, semantic landmarks, descriptive image text, sufficient color contrast, useful validation messages, reduced-motion respect, and mobile controls large enough for comfortable touch use.
