# Documents and payments

Quotes, invoices, and receipts are issued from bookings and receive unique yearly identifiers. The JSON snapshot contains customer, stay, room rates/counts/nights, subtotal, extras, discount, total, amount paid, and balance. PDFs are loaded only in the download route. Authenticated staff can download directly; WhatsApp and email use HMAC-signed links that expire after seven days. Quotes may have validity dates. Receipts are blocked until a payment exists. Payments cannot exceed the outstanding balance and update unpaid, partially-paid, or paid state. Resend delivery is idempotent per document and recipient and is recorded in the activity log.
